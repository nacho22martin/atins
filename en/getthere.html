Getting Here

Atins is located on the sought after tourist corridor that covers Jericoacoara in Ceará, the Parnaiba river delta in Piaui and the Lençóis Maranhenses in Maranhão; an astonishing circuit of thrills throughout the best natural wonders of Brazil`s North East.

You can reach the village of Atins in a number of ways:

From São Luís

Minibus (approx. 4 hrs): One of the best options since they pick you up from your hotel, house or the airport, and take you straight to Barreirinhas. Price: r$ 40 per person. Contacts: Fanttur +55 98 3236 1608 / 3082 3300 / 9976 8734 - Brtur +55 98 3236 6056 / 9114 5813 / 9903 071 brtur@terra.com.br – Levatur +55 98 9969 4544

Bus (approx. 5 hrs): The bus leaves from the bus station (rodoviária) in São Luís, located near the airport. It may be handy when coming straight from the airport, otherwise getting from the centre of town to the rodoviária will need to include a taxi ride to the station. Price: r$ 40 per person. Contacts: Cisne Branco +55 98 4345 1233 / 3243 2847 www.cisnebrancoturismo.com.br

Group Taxi (approx. 4 hrs): These are taxis run by a coop and are shared with other passengers. They pick you up from your hotel. Price: r$ 50 per person based on 4 sharing. Contacts: Taxi cooperative (São Luis) +55 98 3258 9239 - (Barreirinhas) +55 98 3349 1511 www.coopcart.com.br

Private Taxi (approx. 3 hrs): You can charter one of the coop taxis just for you and your travel group. Price: r$ 200 maximum 4 people.

Small Airplane: Usually hotels in São Luis can arrange these transportations for you.

Once in Barreirinhas

Speedboat (approx. 1 hr): By far the best way of getting here since it is a lovely ride throughout the Preguiças River. Price: r$ 50/70 per person depending on the number of passengers and bargaining. Just ask at the river port.

Tourist Speedboat (approx. 3/4 hrs): Same as the previous one but with stop-off points to be agreed on some nearby sites like Vassouras, Mandacarú and Caburé. Price: r$ 70/100 per person depending on the number of passengers and bargaining. Just ask at the river port.

Public 4x4 Jeep (approx. 2 hrs): This is a cheap way to get to Atins but a bumpy ride throughout inland sand tracks. They usually leave at 9/10 am and 14/15 pm although timetables are subject to changes. Price: r$ 20 per person. The departure point is in different streets in the city center. Just ask the local shops for information.

From Jericoacoara

4×4 Jeep (approx. 7 hrs): It is a one day trip from Jericoacoara to Atins right through different palm trees groves, sand dunes and mangroves mainly on dirt roads. Price: r$1200 up to 4 passengers. Contact: Mr. Dadinho at Jeri Moon agency will arrange this for you +55 88 8816 1611 / +55 85 9954 1938 jerimoontur@hotmail

Tourist 4×4 Jeep (approx. 2/3 days): Same as the previous one but with several tour stops to be agreed alongside the Jericoacoara and Lençóis Maranhenses strip. Most of them will do a sleepover in Parnaiba with tours on the river Delta. Price: r$1500/2000 up to 4 passengers.

Public Transportation (approx. 2 days): It is a journey alternating different public transportation such as buses, 4x4 jeeps and boats. You can get up to Caburé and from there take a boat to Atins or go up to Barreirinhas and from there choose one of the different options to get to the Village. An overnight stay most likely will need to be done either in Parnaiba, Tutoia or Paulino Neves. Price: r$150 per person.
