Atins – Lençóis Maranhenses

Lençóis Maranhenses

The Lençóis Maranhenses is one of the most amazing ecosystems in the world. It is the only desert of the planet with thousands of crystal clear rainwater lagoons. The park has more than 155,000 hectares of a surrealistic landscape: a white sand ocean splashed by thousands of green and blue lagoons, in which small fish, crustaceans and turtles, abound.

Lagoons are born anew each year after the January-to-June rains fill the valleys between the dunes. In early July, at their fullest, some of these lagoons are more than 300 feet long and up to ten feet deep. Once the rainy season ends, the lagoons begin to evaporate in the equatorial heat, although the biggest ones will hold water all year round.

Atins

Atins is a small fishermen village located on the coast of Maranhão and surrounded by the dunes of the Lençóis Maranhenses National Park, the delta of the Preguiças river, a small creek (Igarapé), and the Atlantic Ocean. The village consists of a small group of cottages and simple houses sparsely lined up along few sand streets. With endless dunes, wide sandy beaches, warm shallow waters, swaying palm trees and a constant soothing sea breeze, Atins is the perfect destination to enjoy a unique peace and quietness.

For those who enjoy being in contact with nature, Atins is the best base camp for the Lençóis Maranhenses, due to its proximity to the dunes and its vast coastline. Getting here takes less than one hour by a breathtaking speedboat ride through the Preguiças river from the main town and transport hub Barreirinhas.

It is important for you to know that there are no banks and cash machines in Atins. What`s more, none of the pousadas, restaurants and shops accept credit cards due to the village´s limited phone and internet connection. Thus, bring enough cash for your expenses. It is highly recommended to exchange your foreign currency into Brazilian Real in major cities, such as Sao Luiz or Fortaleza.
