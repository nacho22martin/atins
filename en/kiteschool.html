Atins Kite School

Atins Kiteboarding is teamed by a small community of passionate riders. Our staff of qualified and enthusiastic instructors will teach you how to ride fast and safe. Atins is the best choice for beginners interested in taking their first steps due to its unbelievable constant winds, huge flat lagoons with shallow warm waters and uncrowded beaches.

The Kite School is comfortably situated on Atins main road with straight beach front access and under a magnificent shady coconut grove. Among the various kite amenities, Atins Kiteboarding offers individual and group lessons in English, Portuguese and Spanish, equipment rental, storage and kite caddy services, facilities for cleaning and drying your gear and large open spaces to chill out in between sessions.

If in need of renting kite gear it is recommended to let us know in advance.

Downwind & Kite Tours

Several options of guided downwind and kite tours are arranged depending on your level of riding and personal kite style preferences. Every alternative offers land transportation, rescue boat as well as the possibility of being accompanied by non riding travel partners.

Beginners and intermediate riders downwind through huge flat lagoons whilst the more experienced can venture out to the open sea, where you might find 1.5 – 2 meters waves at the right tide. Other options include downwinds through the calm waters of the Preguiças River as well as kiteboarding in the inland lagoons of the Lençóis Maranhenses National Park.
